Test BMA camera access

iPhone users: Firefox is recommended. Safari may not open BMA Traffic's external HTTP-only camera page reliably. Open Bus-287 in Firefox before running this test.

BMA Traffic is a separate, HTTP-only website. Its availability and content are controlled by BMA Traffic, not Bus-287.

  1. Open the test. Tap the blue BMA camera button below.
  2. Allow the external page. If your browser shows a warning, continue only if you accept opening BMA's HTTP website.
  3. Check the camera. Wait for the BMA page and confirm whether its camera content appears.
  4. Return to Bus-287. Come back to this browser tab.
  5. Report the result. Select “Yes” or “No” below to enter Bus Watch.

How to use Bus-287

  1. Choose your location using GPS or by tapping the map.
  2. Choose a nearby bus stop.
  3. Choose the bus route you want to follow.
  4. Choose a live bus from the list.
  5. View live bus details and open an available BMA camera.

Use the labeled bar at the bottom to hide or reopen the panel while viewing the map.
